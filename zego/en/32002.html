Childhood memories aroused! Still remember Broken Gigant in Bakusou Kyoudai Lets Go?

Made of ABS, Side Stepper is strong and powerful with torque dynamics, obviously superior to other remote control vehicles.

Two-way roller in roller, the way the wheels are designed enables Side Stepper to move in various ways in a small area.

Apart from going forward, backward, left and right, Side Stepper can spin on the spot in both directions. It can also make lateral movement both left and right and that’s exactly how it gets its name.

Wheels are controlled by a single motor, designed to run as fast as 5km/h.

A renewable AA alkaline battery provides stable walk for 30 minutes.

27.145MHZ superconductive radio control and strong anti-interference capacity allow players to control Side Stepper from as far as 30m away.

Rubber tires in Side stepper add extra "bite" on the roads, with which Side stepper can walk on floors of different materials, including cement, wood, tiles and carpets.
