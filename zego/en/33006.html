TalkieBuddy not only looks like a real parrot, but also talks and sings, a perfect match to the living room.

When the player blows air to TalkieBuddy’s abdomen or whistles, it will tweet or sing, with so much classical music to keep the player refreshed.

TalkieBuddy is more than an MP3. It is a lifelike electronic parrot, who can echo your words.

Press the recording button on TalkieBuddy’s abdomen and it will record and replay, just like a real parrot.

A group of TalkieBuddies singing together brings a lot more fun.

Unlimited TalkieBuddies in sync can tweet or sing together in the same time as long as one leading TalkieBuddy is blown to or whistled. Children will sure enjoy themselves singing along.

Automatic sleeping mode for saving energy, long standby without charge.

TalkieBuddy will fall “asleep” and stay still when it receives no instructions or action in 10 minutes. A press on the button of TalkieBuddy’s abdomen will wake it up.

Thoughtful design, low battery automatically alert

When TalkieBuddy is in low battery, its tweeting warning sound will automatically remind the player to replace a new battery.
