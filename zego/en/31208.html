Different from the traditional wheeled design, Airjet is driven by wind wheels and can be as fast as lightening.

Due to wheel rub and kinetic energy loss of the four-wheeled drive shafts, traditional wheeled remote control vehicles can not reach a high speed. Zego creatively makes the driving mode of an airplane work on a remote control vehicle.

Airjet is more than a vehicle. It is also a simple UFO!

When the wind turbine is dismantled, Airjet becomes an aero vehicle and can take off and land directly.

The gun-shaped remote control will for sure become children’s favourite.

A 716# coreless motor with up to 40,000 rpm in rotating speed

An Airjet fast as lightening will outrun other remote control vehicles easily!

The soft propeller design increases safety on Airjet.
