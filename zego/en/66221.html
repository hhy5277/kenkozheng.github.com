With a removable 480-pixel HD camera, Zeraxa can take pictures and videos from far away through the remote control.

Image size of 1280 x1024 JPG; video resolution of 640 x480 AVI

Equipped with 3 different lights, Zeraxa shines as a twinkling UFO in the dark.

Bright white on the forearm, green on the back and red for the head lamp.

8520 coreless motor with up to 50,000 rpm in rotating speed provides the most powerful force.

A rechargeable 530mAH lithium battery enables Zeraxa to fly as long as 7 minutes.

A 2.4G frequency hopping controller and 80m control distance allow players to better enjoy themselves.

Equipped with high precision compasses in both remote control and fuselage, Zeraxa fixes its “eyes” on the player.

When flying in intelligent orientation mode, players will not be puzzled by the flying directions of the drone.

Both newbies and masters can find their way to fun with high and low velocity adjustable.

Default speed is in low velocity, 60% of the maximum speed. Press “High Speed”, and you will immediately get what a master deserves-High Speed Mode.
