ABOUT ZEGO

Founded in 2000, Zego is an innovative electronic toy company dedicated to developing small aircraft, radio-controlled cars and other radio remote control products. To make the dream come true for the masses has always been Zego’s mission. Since its establishment, Zego leads people to appreciate the beauty of the world in a new perspective, with its quick R&D response, innovative and advanced technology, thoughtful and easy-to-operate design, so that science and technology are no longer as cold as iron, and everyone can easily become a radio player.

WHY ZEGO?

As one of China’s high-end innovative electronic toy companies, Zego hires more than 500 employees including top designers, researchers and operators from around the world. With the help of the latest generation of SLA rapid prototyping machines, large vacuum machines and other advanced processing units, Zego has so far, based on market demand, released glider, drones, helicopters, radiofrequency-controlled cars, flying saucers and other electronic toys, one of which breaks a Guinness World Record for being the smallest remote control plane model in the world. Besides, all of Zego’s products are ISO 9001 certified, accredited by ICTI and meet the requirements of the RoHS Directive of the EU, thus becoming best-selling products in American and European countries.
